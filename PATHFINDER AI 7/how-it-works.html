The Problem Space

Why Pathfinder AI Was Created

Modern hiring relies heavily on automated screening filters called **Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)**. These platforms scan incoming CVs using basic keyword-matching rules. If your professional history uses non-standard titles or misses precise technical terms, the software filters it out before a human ever views it.

Pathfinder AI was engineered to flip this balance of power. By using identical text processing flows, it analyzes your document from an objective mathematical perspective, ensuring your professional layout remains highly resilient against machine filters.

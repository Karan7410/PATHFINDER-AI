The Methodology

How Pathfinder Engine Computes Career Vectors

Pathfinder AI operates at the intersection of **Transformer-based Natural Language Processing (NLP)** and market telemetry. Standard applications parse text as simple keyword strings; Pathfinder interprets resumes as high-dimensional, contextual semantic profiles.

Semantic Entity Recognition: Distinguishes tools, frameworks, responsibilities, and leadership scales rather than treating text as generic strings.

Graph-Based Role Mapping: Employs structured ontology trees mapping modern roles (e.g., DevOps Engineer) to exact parent dependencies (Cloud Architecture, CI/CD, Containerization Systems).
